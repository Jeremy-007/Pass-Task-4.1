Vaccine 1 - Moderna COVID-19 Vaccine




What is Moderna COVID-19 Vaccine?

Moderna COVID-19 vaccine is a two-shot vaccine which need to have the second injection after 28 days for the first one. According to The WHO Strategic Advisory Group of Experts (SAGA), it is advised that this vaccine can be take by people who are 18 years or older.

Vaccine Information

WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW BEFORE YOU GET THIS VACCINE

  • Technology Used
  • Effectiveness
  • Benefits



Technology

The Moderna COVID-19 Vaccine includes the following ingredients:

  • messenger ribonucleic acid(mRNA)
  • lipids (SM-102, polyethelene glycol [PEG] 2000 dimyristoyl [DMG], cholesterol, and 1,2-distearoyl-sn-glycero-3-photosphocholine [DSPC])
  • Tromethamine
  • tromethamine hydrochloride
  • acedic acid
  • sodium acetate trihydrate
  • sucrose

Effectiveness

Based on evidence from clinical trials, the Moderna vaccine was 94.1% effective at preventing laboratory-confirmed COVID-19 illness in people who received two doses who had no evidence of being previously infected.

Benefits

mRNA vaccines are a new type of vaccine to protect against infectious diseases. To trigger an immune response, many vaccines put a weakened or inactivated germ into our bodies. Not mRNA vaccines. Instead, they teach our cells how to make a protein—or even just a piece of a protein—that triggers an immune response inside our bodies. That immune response, which produces antibodies, is what protects us from getting infected if the real virus enters our bodies.

Side Effect

Side effects that have been reportd with the Moderna COVID-19 Vaccine Include:

Injection Side reactions:

  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Swelling

General Side Effects:

  • Tiredness
  • Headache
  • Muscle pain
  • Chills
  • Fever
  • Nausea
