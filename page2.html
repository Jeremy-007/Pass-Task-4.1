Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 Vaccine

What is Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine?

"The Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine is the first to receive emergency validation from the World Health Organization (WHO)on 31 December 2020.It also was granted Emergency Use Authorization (EDA) by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration on February 11 December 2020 and Conditional Marketing Authorization(CMA) from the European Commission on 21 December 2020.

Technology Used

The Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 Vaccine includes the following ingredients:

  • mRNA
  • lipids ((4-hydroxybutyl)azanediyl)bis(hexane-6,1-diyl)bis(2-hexyldecanoate))
  • 2 [(polyethylene glycol)-2000]-N,N-ditetradecylacetamide, 1,2-Distearoyl-sn-glycero-3- phosphocholine, and cholesterol)
  • potassium chloride
  • monobasic potassium phosphate
  • 0.9% sodium chloride (normal saline, preservative-free
  • dibasic sodium phospate dihydrate
  • sucrose

The Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 Vaccine is administered as a 2-dose series, 3 weeks apart, into the muscle.

Effectiveness

The Pfizer-BioNTech vaccine was 95% effective in clinical trials (efficacy) at preventing laboratory-confirmed COVID-19 illness in people without evidence of previous infection.

Who should get the Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine:

  • recommeded for individuals 16 years of age and older.

Who should NOT the Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine::

  • had a severe allergic reaction after a previous dose of this vaccine.
  • had a severe allergic reaction to any ingredient of this vaccine.

Benefits

In an ongoing clinical trial, the Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 Vaccine has been shown to prevent COVID-19 following 2 doses given 3 weeks apart. The duration of protection against COVID-19 is currently unknown.

Side Effects

Side effects that have been reported with the Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 Vaccine include:

Injection site reactions:

  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Swelling

General side effects:

  • Tiredness
  • Headache
  • Muscle pain
  • Chills
  • Fever
  • Nausea
